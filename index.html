Ball of Fluff

This adult-humored narrative portrays a person battling anxiety, depression, and loneliness during the pandemic, who seeks comfort in a pet store. He adopts an indoor cat to help cope with the loss of a loved one. However, the feline’s constant need for attention and developing real-world dynamics trigger beautiful tensions, showing that owning a pet is a deeply valuable life responsibility.

David Alejandro (Author)

Frat Boy

A gripping coming-of-conscience novel tracking Jared Jimenez, a first-generation Latino student who views his high fraternity status as his ultimate armor. When he meets Monica Rodriguez, a politically fearless student voice, he is thrust directly into campus friction. When a complex secret links Monica back to his own brotherhood house, loyalty suddenly morphs into something highly dangerous.
